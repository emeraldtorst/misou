01

The Philosophy

Reshaping boundaries. Located in the historic heart of Vienna’s first district, MISO·U represents a curated dining destination where contemporary Asian techniques intersect with classic European elegance.

Our culinary canvas is defined by contrast. From the quiet warmth of brushed dark slate and smoked amber glass to the bold complexity of fermented misos, local Austrian ingredients, and rare Eastern botanicals, we craft an atmosphere that is sensory, spacious, and deeply cinematic.

02

Culinary Signature

An editorial presentation of our signature creations. Resolute focus on depth, texture, and visual balance.

Signature Sea

Salmon A La Miso

Grilled salmon fillet served with baby pak choi, sliced onion, crisp carrots, zucchini, broccoli, and snow peas, drizzled in our house specialty miso reduction, served with steamed jasmine rice.

Pairing: Grüner Veltliner Smaragd, Domäne Wachau
Signature Land

Crispy Soy Duck

Oven-roasted duck breast with a crispy skin, resting on a bed of fresh pak choi, tender onions, and soy sprouts, topped with a rich caramelized soy reduction, served with steamed jasmine rice.

Pairing: Pinot Noir Bourgogne Rouge, Faiveley
03

The Lounge & Libations

An evening dining experience extends far beyond the plate. Our lounge offers a quiet sanctuary of smoked glass, warm metal accents, and rare vintages.

Our cellar bridges continents. Discover an extraordinary selection of organic Austrian wines, Grand Cru Champagnes, and small-batch Japanese sakes sourced directly from boutique brewers in Kyoto and Niigata. Each sake is selected for its clean minerality to complement our rich fusion flavors.

East-West Pairings Curated sake & wine flights adjusted weekly.
Botanical Cocktails House infusions using fresh shiso leaf, yuzu juice, and smoked wood.

Weekday Lunch Ritual
